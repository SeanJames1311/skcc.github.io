Cooking Class

To be able to cook is one of the many talents that are lost in this generation. More low income families are buying fast food instead of cooking their own nutritious meals because they simply do not know how to. With our renowned cooking classes, we help to jumpstart families with basic cooking skills in our facilities that have been funded by Sobeys, Loblaws and other supermarket chains. Families will be able to spend more quality time with each other, while also making delicious, nutritious meals to enjoy. Classes start April 2/2017 and run to June 25/2017. Book your session(s) now
