Taekwondo Classes


Self defense is an important & necessary skill to learn, especially in this community, as crime is a serious issue here.

Equip yourself with the tactital skills needed to protect yourself and others.

Become the hero you were meant to be.

Class age groups are from 10-12,13-17, and 18-50.

Classes start from 50$/person and registration begins November 25th/2016. All classes start March 6/2017. Classes require at least 1 year commitment
