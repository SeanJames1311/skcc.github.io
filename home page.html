Welcome to Seneca King Community Centre


Previous Next

Hi! I'm Rajiv Patel. 4 years ago, I was a just an aimless teen, bouncing in and out of juvenile detention. I didn't have anywhere to go, and all my so called friends abandoned me. Then I found the Seneca King Community Centre. Or I see it, they found me. With their many programs, I was able to establish a great social circle and build lasting friendships that would ever change my life. Today, I run a startup internet company that I helped create with the same friends at SKCC. I owe it all to the good folks at Seneca King Community Centre. Thank you SKCC!
