Dance Class


Dancing is the ultimate form of human expression and what better way to learn & experience the finer styles of dance, than right here at Seneca King. We pride ourselves on equipping the facility with dance instructors from all walks of earth, who are able to teach the various intricacies of dance.

Our classes include hip hop, jazz, ballet, fitness, ballroom and cultural.

Classes are 10$/person and start from March 12/2017 and run to May 7/2017. Ignite the passion burning within you!
