Drama Club


Life without the arts would simply be dull. We at Seneca King strongly believe in that statement and we are dedicated to bringing the joy of performance to the hearts of every individual.

With our state of the art theatre stage and our committed drama coaches, we are aiming to ignite the passion of performance in all.

Our age groups are divided from 6-11, 12-16, and 17-65.

Registration closes January 7, 2016. Classes run from April 9/2017 to June 26/2017.
