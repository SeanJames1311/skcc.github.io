Garden Club


Nature is beautiful. Tending to nature even more so. However, many have forgotten how to care for nature. The benefits to garden club include, and are not limited to, peace within and peace towards others.

In our club, particpants will be growing a greenhouse for the city and tending to to other produce. They will learn the fundamentals of nurturing plant life, and how to properly take care of each kind of produce.

This club is all ages and anyone can join. Registration begins November 25/2016
